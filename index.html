About Us

Skills Improvement foundation is a non-profit organization incorporated under section 42 of securities and exchange commission of Pakistan. The objective of this organization is to improve economic growth of Pakistan, alleviating poverty and livelihood standards by promoting skills, cottage industry and entrepreneurship. Step right is a project of Skills improvement foundation in education sector, also SIF aims to promote entrepreneurship culture by providing interest free loans to skilled people and mentoring them will all necessary expertise required in setting up their own enterprises till they sustain.
